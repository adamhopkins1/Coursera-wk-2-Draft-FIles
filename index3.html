Background

Ultimate Frisbee or what is now called "Ultimate" has been around in some fashion since the 1960's.Joel Silver (No Relation to the Hollywood Producer) is credited with introducing the game to his High School in 1968 after learning about the game at summer camp. The game graudually began to spread to other campuses soon afterward.

The Ultimate Players Association was orginally founded in 1979 and began organinzing tournaments across the country. In 2010, the UPA began organizing tournaments across the country. In 2010, UPA became the USA Ultimate

Other than professional tournaments, games of Ultimate are typically played without referees. Instead, players rely on "The Spirit of the Game" to encourage sportsmanship and self-officiating.
